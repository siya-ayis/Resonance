# RESONANCE — Demo Video Script (≈5 min, story-driven)

**Tone arc:** start slow + emotional → build energetic → end inspiring.
**Golden rules:** open captions on every line · use ONE high-bass song · show the vibration *visibly* (the rice/water trick) · never name libraries or over-explain tech.
**To trim to ~3 min:** keep HOOK → SOLUTION → CORE IDEA → FEEL → CLOSE; drop PLAY and CREATE.

---

## 🎤 0:00 – 0:30 · HOOK  (the most important 30 seconds)
**Tone:** slow, quiet, emotional. Let each line breathe.

🎙️ **Say:**
"Imagine a world where music… is silent.
For over 1.5 billion people, that isn't imagination — it's everyday life.
Music is the most universal thing we share… and yet millions are locked out of it."

*(beat)*

🎙️ **Punchline:**
"So we asked one question — what if you could *feel* music, *see* it, and *understand* it… without ever hearing a sound?"

🎥 **Show:** someone putting on headphones, then nothing — a flat, lifeless waveform / plain captions scrolling by. Cold, grey, empty.

---

## 🔥 0:30 – 1:00 · THE SOLUTION
**Tone:** warm, confident — the light switching on.

🎙️ **Say:**
"This is Resonance — a free, AI-powered way to experience music through light and touch, built for the Deaf and hard-of-hearing.
We don't try to fix hearing. We reimagine music itself."

🎥 **Show:** logo blooms like ink in water → cut to the app opening, color flowing on screen.

---

## ⚙️ 1:00 – 1:30 · THE CORE IDEA  (your "judge clarity" sentence)
**Tone:** clear and deliberate — slow down for the key line.

🎙️ **Say (this is THE line — say it cleanly):**
"Our AI separates a song into its individual instruments — bass, drums, melody, voice — and turns each one into its own color, motion, and vibration."

🎙️ **Then:**
"Most apps blur everything into one blob. We give every instrument its own identity — so you can finally tell them apart with your eyes and your hands."

🎥 **Show:** a single waveform splitting into four labeled streams — 🟣 bass · 🟡 drums · 🟢 melody · 🔵 vocals.

---

## 🚀 1:30 – 3:00 · HERO DEMO ▸ FEEL  (your winning section — slow down, spend time here)
**Tone:** building energy. This is where they *get it*.

🎥 **Step 1 — load a song.**
🎙️ "Let's play a real song through Resonance."

🎥 **Step 2 — music starts, narrate what they see.**
🎙️ (talk over the visuals, calm and rhythmic):
"The bass becomes a vibration you feel…
the drums burst as flashes of energy…
the melody flows as motion…
and the voice becomes the glowing center of it all."

🎥 **Step 3 — THE MONEY MOMENT.** Show the phone vibrating *visibly* — grains of rice dancing on it, or a glass of water rippling beside it. Then put a hand on the phone right before the drop.
🎙️ (let the drop hit):
"And you don't just *see* it…"

*(drop lands — screen explodes in color, the rice jumps, the hand flinches)*

"…you feel it. Right in your hands.
This isn't a visualizer. It's music, translated into something the human body can actually feel."

🎥 **Step 4 — show lyrics + emotion shift.**
🎙️:
"It carries the words too — and even the *feeling*. As the mood of the song changes, the colors change with it… turning sound into a living, visual story."

---

## 🎮 3:00 – 3:40 · PLAY MODE  (interaction — short, fun, energetic)
**Tone:** light and playful.

🎙️ **Say:**
"And Resonance isn't just something you watch — it's something you play.
Tap any instrument and feel how different each one really is — a heavy kick, a sharp hat, a soft, swelling pad.
You don't just learn what music looks like. You learn how it *feels*."

🎥 **Show:** fingers tapping the instrument pads, each one a different color + a different buzz.

---

## 🎨 3:40 – 4:10 · CREATE / THE FUTURE
**Tone:** hopeful, forward-looking.

🎙️ **Say:**
"And this is just the beginning. Soon, users won't only experience music —
they'll *create* it: building songs through touch, color, and movement.
For the first time, a Deaf person could compose a song… and feel every note of it."

🎥 **Show:** a quick mock of CREATE mode.

---

## 🌍 4:10 – 4:40 · IMPACT + MICROSOFT MISSION
**Tone:** grounded, sincere.

🎙️ **Say:**
"Resonance lives Microsoft's mission — to empower *every* person to achieve more.
By making music accessible, we open doors to creativity, connection, and culture.
This isn't just technology. It's inclusion, powered by AI."

🎥 **Show:** the listener from the opening — now smiling, hand on the phone, color glowing.

---

## 🧠 4:40 – 5:00 · CLOSING  (slow, let it land)
**Tone:** quiet, powerful.

🎙️ **Say:**
"Music was never meant to be heard alone.
With Resonance, it can be felt… seen… and lived —
by everyone."

🎥 **Last frame:** logo + tagline *"feel the drop."* + (optional) "Microsoft Global Hackathon · Team [name]".

# 🧠 DELIVERY TIPS (this is what wins it)
**Tone strategy:** open slow + emotional → middle energetic → close inspiring.

**Demo rules (critical):**
- Use only **1 song** (2 max). Pick one with a **big, clear bass drop**.
- **Show the drop moment** clearly — build up to it.
- **Show the vibration visibly** — rice grains jumping on the phone, or a glass of water rippling. This is the single most convincing shot. Don't skip it.

**Avoid these mistakes:**
- ❌ Don't over-explain the tech.
- ❌ Don't list every feature — pick FEEL as the star.
- ❌ Don't say "we built this with X library / Y framework."
- ❌ Don't rush the hook — those first 30 seconds win or lose the room.

We give every instrument its own identity — so you can finally tell them apart with your eyes and your hands.

Let's play a real song through Resonance. The bass becomes a vibration you feel… the drums burst as flashes of energy… the melody flows as motion… and the voice becomes the glowing center of it all.

And you don't just see it… you feel it. Right in your hands. This isn't a visualizer. It's music, translated into something the human body can actually feel.

It carries the words too — and even the feeling. As the mood of the song changes, the colors change with it… turning sound into a living, visual story.

And Resonance isn't just something you watch — it's something you play. Tap any instrument and feel how different each one really is — a heavy kick, a sharp hat, a soft, swelling pad. You don't just learn what music looks like. You learn how it feels.

And this is just the beginning. Soon, users won't only experience music — they'll create it: building songs through touch, color, and movement. For the first time, a Deaf person could compose a song… and feel every note of it.

Resonance lives Microsoft's mission — to empower every person to achieve more. By making music accessible, we open doors to creativity, connection, and culture. This isn't just technology. It's inclusion, powered by AI.

Music was never meant to be heard alone. With Resonance, it can be felt… seen… and lived — by everyone."
